The afternoon began with the long awaited visit to the onsen. \u00a0For the guys that meant Gary, David and me. It was a great time of relaxing and getting to know each other better and soaking in some kind of chemically enriched water.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

While we were in the onsen, this bright young university student came in and sat next to me. \u00a0We had a great conversation and I met him again in the park adjoining the onsen where he was with his mother.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

I had a hard time at first believing that she was really old enough to actually be his mother. \u00a0His Japanese name was Joey; this was the first time I had met a young man with that name here in Japan. \u00a0Joey seemed quite interested in trying out his English so I introduced him to Mosese.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

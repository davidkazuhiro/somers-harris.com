However, due to her life experiences with POTS, which is an ongoing battle, Mary\u2019s life has not been a daily experience of Joy. \u00a0Somehow though in the midst of her sufferings Mary seems to be able to exude and bring Joy to almost everyone she meets. \u00a0Her life experiences and hidden illness have given her a heart of deep compassion which results in unceasing efforts to reach out to and stand up for the marginalized and oppressed.<\/p>\n

Both of these are deeply entrenched in the story of Mary and Elizabeth from the Bible narrative. In Church history it was first observed \u00a0by the Franciscians from before 1263 and soon its observance spread throughout the entire Church. The Franciscian order was known for reaching out to the poor and oppressed people of the world.<\/p>\n

He has filled the hungry with good things but has sent the rich away empty.\u201d \u00a0So Mary\u2019s life has in many ways encompassed both the Joy and the suffering \u00a0that is discovered in the full story of her birthday.<\/p>\n

There \u00a0is quite a story that goes with this T-shirt . \u00a0A long time ago when Rhonda and I were living in the Shinagawa section of Tokyo city, we took time to prayer walk around our \u00a0neighbourhood, seeing what we could learn about the area we lived in. \u00a0 Each time we went out to pray we also discovered a new coffee shop to relax in and reflect on our time.<\/p>\n

On one of the prayer walks we passed by a used clothing store and hanging outside in the front was this T-shirt. \u00a0I knew right away that I wanted to buy that shirt. \u00a0Not because of my experiences or passion for surfing. \u00a0– one little known fact about me is that I went surfing \u00a0on the famous Bondai beach near Sydney. \u00a0I\u2019m happy to say that I never fell off of my surf board but then again, I never got to stand up on it either.<\/p>\n

<\/a>I went back to the store a couple of days later fully intending to buy the T-shirt. \u00a0However when I got to the store I could not see it. \u00a0 I entered and looked around and eventually was able to find this T-shirt and buy it. \u00a0 Probably the most I have spent on a T-shirt in a very, very long time.<\/p>\n
